About Nicole

Welcome! My name is Nicole D’Andrea and I’m thrilled that you’re here. I’m a Seasoned Yoga Instructor, Multi-Six-Figure Studio Owner and MINDBODY-Certified Business Consultant. I’m passionate about helping other women in business embrace the strategic aspect of their studios because I know how well building authentic systems, a winning team and a cohesive brand lends itself to the flexible schedule and highly profitable studios we all set out to create in the first place.

I’ve grown from a staff of just five teachers to a team of 13 instructors, two massage therapists, four desk staff members and two managers. I built my 19-mat studio in a highly-competitive, “saturated” market into a six-figure business in less than a year’s time. Our community is thriving. These days, I’m in the studio one or two days a week and I teach because I want to. I get to practice my own yoga and take plenty of time off without worrying about what’s happening at the studio or checking my bank balance. It’s not luck, magic or timing. It’s structure, communication, and balance. I want to share it all with you.

I love being a yoga business coach and I’ve spent countless hours immersing myself in our industry’s best practices and the latest market trends. I’ve almost never been without a mentor or coach. I’m particularly interested in the unique intersection between mindfulness and entrepreneurship because they. are. not. mutually exclusive! I want female founders to ditch the shame, doubt, and fear and feel empowered by their businesses. I want them to know exactly how they can use their gifts to build a thriving wellness community that changes lives and generates major profit. It’s all about balance.
